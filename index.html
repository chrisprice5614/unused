Hello there! My name is Christian Price, but most people just call me Chris. I'm a musician from Spokane Valley, Washington who specializes in the marching arts. Although Marching music is where I put a lot of my time in, I'm also well equipped to write and arrange for other styles and ensembles. I've written original pieces for schools across the planet to play and have written arrangements that have been performed by professional organizations in other countries. Lots of my time is dedicated to researching how to become a better writer, while also learning by taking the time to write many arrangements and original pieces. Currently I'm a senior at Whitworth University, getting a degree in music. I also have a strong interest in computer science! This website you're currently browsing was coded by me! Ever since I was little I've been interested in game development, so after a very long time, I began to develop my own video games. Within the last year I've been trying to merge the realm of music with the realm of computer science, by programing synthesizers and AI that will write its own music.
