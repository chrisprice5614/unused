The Iron Curtain

Preview

General Info

Written by: Christian Price
This piece was made in rememberance of the Berlin Wall and the difficulties that arose for many people because of it. One can hear the wall being built as well as the effects of war while listening.

Grade 3

This piece has lots of rounds, clusters, and polyrhythms that give an extra challenge.
