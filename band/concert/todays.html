General Info

Written by: Christian Price
This piece was made to highlight the imagination one could have while dreaming. Inspired by the events of Coraline, the audience is taken through a land of amazement and terror as things begin to unfold.

Grade 3

There are a couple lines in this show that some students may find difficult. The percussion section, especially the front ensemble, will be given difficult rhythms and polyrhythms. This show is meant for students who have a strong amount of experience on their instruments.
